## Project Overview

### Why
- Build a RAG-based AI Podcast SaaS that curates and narrates daily tech and cybersecurity news.
- Demonstrate practical, end-to-end AI systems engineering (data ingestion, LLM scripting, TTS, storage, and RAG).
- Serve as a high-quality portfolio piece for a CS student focused on AI and Security.

### Who
- CS students and early-career engineers interested in AI and cybersecurity.
- Busy professionals who want a concise daily security briefing in audio form.
- Builders who want a reference implementation of a modern AI media pipeline.

### Value
- Saves time by delivering a daily, curated cybersecurity briefing.
- Provides grounded, citeable summaries via RAG for trust and accuracy.
- Showcases a production-ready AI workflow suitable for hiring portfolios.
